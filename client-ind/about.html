Exceeding Sustainability

DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE


Indmark’s commitment to minimise the impact of its activities on the environment has led to the development of Square Fiber Drums in 2017. A product range that lessens the environmental impact of both production and distribution, Square Fiber Drums are a crucial development in our pursuit of excellence and environmental sustainability.
Since our outset in 1992, Indmark has set new industry standards in manufacturing Cable Trays, Erection Materials and Hot-Dip Galvanizing. We join forces with a global clientele to develop products that bear the highest quality standards. And we believe that our Square Fiber Drums will continue to deliver with that devotion.

Narendra Meher
Managing Director
INDMARK Group

About Us

Since its inception in 1992, when Indmark began just as a dealership, it has advanced today into a novel brand and a chosen service provider for a vast clientele in the business. Diversifying on our dedicated wings - Indmark Formtech, Indmark Galvanizers and Indmark paper form, We currently serve over 1000 clients in 8 countries, and cater to every single infrastructural industrial domain, manufacturing or service. An overwhelming number of client approvals and appreciations, along with our ISO-9001-2008 quality standard constantly motivate us to excel in quality, deliver in time and be competitive in pricing.

With distinctive, proficient methodologies and object-oriented task force, Indmark has continually surpassed service expectations, making it the house of choice for sterling associates such as Larsen & Toubro, Reliance, Suzlon, TATA Projects and numerous others. Being certified with ISO 9001 :2008 and Dun & Bradstreet Global Database, We bring an indigenous interpretation to streamline the quality of all important aspects of our products, right from the raw material resources, product preparation and procedure execution- towards the enhancement of our extensive product range.
